Welcome to the Home of Chuck Cleason

Get ready for some amazing weirdness!!!

Chuck Cleason has been in the worlds of Harry Potter, Frodo Baggins, Aelin Galathynius, Eragon Bromson, Clarissa Morgestern, Kendra Sorenson, Jason Walker, Percy Jackson, and Alanna the Lioness.

Here are some facts about Chuck Cleason:

  • Chuck Cleason is a pseudonym. Cassandra Greenleaf, Reyna K Sorenson, Chuck Cleason, and Chuck the Space Butterfly is the same person.
  • The name 'Chuck' was penned by a friend. When Cassandra went to a Bring Your Friend to Karate Day, Cassandra and said friend worked on nunchucks and punches. Cassandra was pretty okay an nunchucks, especially for the first time, so Chuck became Cassandra's nickname.
  • The next day, Chuck's other friend held up an origami butterfly to Chuck's face. Chuck's friends said Chuck looked like a Space Butterfly, so the name, 'Chuck the Space Butterfly' came into existence.
  • Cassandra Greenleaf and Reyna K Sorenson were coined by Chuck.
